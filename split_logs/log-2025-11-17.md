The high-temperature curve appears somewhat discontinuous because the measurement performed a touchdown calibration every 10 minutes. When the temperature changes slowly this is barely noticeable, but since I increased the ramp rate during the sample-screening stage to save time, the gaps look wider. In the upcoming experiments, I would like to disable the touchdown to obtain a smoother curve.
