January 15, 2025 

In the end, a powder diffraction is measured on 4Cl-Mn-BA on the PanalyticalEmpyrean XRD (Mo source) machine in the Physics Lab. The data is sent back to Korean Collaborator. After refinement, the Powder data matches well with the single crystal diffraction data conducted in Korea. It is thus believed that the sample is very pure. (The single phase consist 90% of the signal.) Thus, the failure of Rigaku is intriguing.
